About Us.


MISSION STATEMENT


We are in business for a purpose; to help in boosting the curb of security problems, delivery of high quality customer service, reducing the use of sub-standard solar products that are not durable and pose greater risks, reducing cost on the long run and helping to make a meaningful difference in the fight against climate change with the use of more and better solar powered products.

APPROACH


We offer a wide range of services; providing solar hybrid and complete off grid power solutions, building and operating solar projects at commercial and utility scale/levels, working with partners to bring solar power to their customers’ homes and dealing directly with individuals for their personal use.

OUR PRODUCTS


Solar flood lights, street lights, quality renewable energy equipments, devices & accessories including deep cycle lithium ion batteries, rechargeable fittings & bulb, solar panels & charge controllers and inverters.

WHAT WE DO


Account management of residential partners, individual sales to home and office owners, schools, estates, banks, construction sites, churches and recreational centers, customer service and maintenance support.
